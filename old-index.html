Bashful & Bashful-sh

Bashful-sh is an independent, open-source, and non-profit organization dedicated to developing cutting-edge software solutions. While the team behind Bashful-sh shares members with Easter Company, it operates as a separate entity with its own distinct legal structure, ownership, and licensing. This allows for greater flexibility in project development and community collaboration.

Bashful-sh is committed to creating high-quality, accessible software that empowers users and pushes the boundaries of technology. Our projects are driven by a passion for innovation and a belief in the power of open-source collaboration. We invite developers and users alike to join us in building the future of software together.
